### 4.11 ‚Äî Ammo Tracking

- `hasAmmo(c, weaponName)` / `spendAmmo(c, weaponName)` in `utils.ts`

  exhausted ‚Äî caller (AI) should fall back to melee.

- Zero action cost per RAW (magic item verbal commands).
- Test: defend-profile Giant Fly stand-in moves toward enemy after being commanded.
